Vendor Policies & Code of Conduct

Effective Date: September 26, 2025

Welcome to the LinkUp Gadgets community of sellers! To ensure a safe, trusted, and fair marketplace for everyone, all vendors are required to adhere to the following policies.

1. Product Authenticity and Listing Accuracy

  • Genuine Products Only: All products listed must be authentic. The sale of counterfeit, fake, or replica products is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate and permanent suspension of your account.
  • Accurate Descriptions: Product descriptions, images, and specifications must accurately represent the item for sale. Misleading information is not permitted.
  • Condition: The condition of the product (e.g., new, refurbished) must be clearly stated.

2. Prohibited Items

Vendors are prohibited from listing illegal items, stolen goods, or any products that violate intellectual property rights. A full list of prohibited items is available in our help center.

3. Order Fulfillment and Shipping

  • Timely Shipping: Vendors are expected to process and ship orders within the timeframe specified in their listings. Prompt fulfillment is key to customer satisfaction.
  • Communication: Keep buyers informed about the status of their orders. If there are delays, communicate them proactively.
  • Stock Management: You are responsible for keeping your stock quantities accurate to avoid selling items that are out of stock.

4. Customer Service and Disputes

Provide a high level of customer service. Respond to customer inquiries politely and in a timely manner. In the event of a dispute, LinkUp Gadgets may intervene to mediate a resolution based on the evidence provided by both the buyer and the seller.

5. Payouts

Payouts for fulfilled orders are processed on a rolling basis, typically within 7 business days after the customer has confirmed receipt of the item, minus the standard platform commission. Ensure your payment information is up-to-date to avoid delays.

6. Policy Violations

Violations of these policies may result in a range of actions, including but not limited to: listing removal, temporary or permanent selling restrictions, and account suspension. We are committed to building a trustworthy platform, and we appreciate your cooperation in upholding these standards.
