Our Story

Connecting Ghana's Tech Future

At LinkUp Gadgets, we believe that technology is the great equalizer. Our mission began with a simple idea: create a trusted online marketplace that empowers local electronics vendors and brings the latest technology to every doorstep in Ghana.

We are more than just a marketplace; we are a community. We started LinkUp Gadgets in Accra after noticing how difficult it was for customers to find genuine products without the risk of fraud, and for small business owners to reach a wider audience beyond their physical stores. We provide a platform with secure payments through Paystack, integrated logistics, and the tools for vendors to build their digital storefront with confidence.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most trusted and reliable tech ecosystem in West Africa, fostering economic growth for local entrepreneurs and making technology accessible and affordable for everyone. We are committed to authenticity, security, and exceptional customer service.
