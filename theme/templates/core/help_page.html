Frequently Asked Questions

Delivery times vary depending on your location. For major cities like Accra and Kumasi, delivery typically takes 1-3 business days. For other regions, it may take 3-7 business days. You can always track your order in real-time through your account dashboard.

We offer a 7-day return policy for most items. Products must be returned in their original packaging and in the same condition as received. Some products, such as earphones and certain accessories, may have different return policies for hygiene reasons.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and cash on delivery for selected areas.

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. You'll also receive email and SMS updates about your order status, including a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

Most electronic products come with a manufacturer's warranty. The warranty period varies by product and brand, typically ranging from 6 months to 2 years. Warranty information is provided on each product page and in the documentation that comes with your purchase.
