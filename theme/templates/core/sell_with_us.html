Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about selling on LinkUp Gadgets

How much does it cost to become a vendor?

Registration as a vendor is completely free. We only charge a commission on successful sales, which varies by product category.

How long does the verification process take?

Our verification process typically takes 2-3 business days once all required documents are submitted.

How do I receive payments for my sales?

We process payments to vendors on a bi-weekly basis via bank transfer or mobile money, depending on your preference.

What happens if a customer returns a product?

We handle the return process according to our return policy. If the return is due to product defects, the cost will be deducted from your account.

Can I sell both new and used products?

Yes, you can sell both new and refurbished/used products, but they must be clearly labeled as such and meet our quality standards.
