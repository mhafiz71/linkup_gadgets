About LinkUp Gadgets

Connecting Ghana's Tech Market & Delivering with Ease

Our Story

Founded in 2022, LinkUp Gadgets was born out of a vision to transform how Ghanaians access and purchase technology products. We recognized the challenges that consumers faced: limited access to authentic products, inconsistent pricing, and unreliable delivery services.

Starting with just a small team of tech enthusiasts in Accra, we've grown into Ghana's premier multi-vendor marketplace for technology products. Our journey has been driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

Today, LinkUp Gadgets connects thousands of customers with trusted vendors across the country, offering a wide range of products from smartphones and laptops to smart home devices and accessories.

Our Mission

To provide Ghanaians with seamless access to authentic technology products through a trusted marketplace that empowers both consumers and vendors. We strive to make the latest technology accessible to everyone while supporting local businesses and driving digital inclusion across Ghana.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading technology marketplace, known for reliability, authenticity, and innovation. We envision a future where every Ghanaian has access to quality technology products that enhance their lives, and where local tech vendors thrive in a supportive ecosystem.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at LinkUp Gadgets

Authenticity

We guarantee that all products sold on our platform are genuine and sourced from authorized channels. We have zero tolerance for counterfeit products.

Customer First

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We continuously strive to improve their experience and exceed their expectations.

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency in all our dealings with customers, vendors, and partners. We do what we say and say what we do.

Innovation

We embrace technology and continuously innovate to improve our platform, processes, and services to better serve our community.

Community Impact

We are committed to making a positive impact in Ghana by supporting local businesses, creating jobs, and promoting digital literacy.

Security

We prioritize the security of our platform and protect our customers' data and transactions with the highest standards of security measures.
